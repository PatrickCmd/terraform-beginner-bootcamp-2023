Ugandan Rolex Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Chapati dough by mixing flour, salt, and water. Knead into a smooth dough and divide it into small balls. Roll each ball into thin chapatis.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet with oil, saut√© onions, tomatoes, and green bell pepper. Add the beaten eggs and scramble them.
  4. Take a chapati, spread egg filling, and roll it up. Serve with hot sauce and toppings of your choice.

Tips:

  • Chapatis should be thin and well-cooked but not crispy.
  • Feel free to add other ingredients like cabbage or carrots to the egg filling for variety.
  • Serve with sliced avocado for a creamy twist.
